\begin{center}
Starting with:  
$$C=F + (1-\alpha)B$$
expand and rearrange:\\
$$C=F + B- \alpha B$$
$$C-B=F - \alpha B$$
Because the above relationship holds for all colour channels of both images, this gives us the system
$$\begin{pmatrix}
C^{r}_1 - B^{r}_1 = F^{r} -\alpha B^{r}_1\\
C^{g}_1 - B^{g}_1 = F^{g} -\alpha B^{g}_1\\
...\\
C^{g}_2 - B^{g}_2 = F^{g} -\alpha B^{g}_2\\
C^{b}_2 - B^{b}_2 = F^{b} -\alpha B^{b}_2\\
\end{pmatrix}$$
or
$$\begin{pmatrix}
  C^{r}_1\\
  C^{g}_1\\
  C^{b}_1\\
  C^{r}_2\\
  C^{g}_2\\
  C^{b}_2\\
\end{pmatrix}
-\begin{pmatrix}
  B^{r}_1 \\
  B^{g}_1 \\
  B^{b}_1 \\
  B^{r}_2 \\
  B^{g}_2 \\
  B^{b}_2 \\
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
  F^{r} \\
  F^{g} \\
  F^{b} \\
  F^{r} \\
  F^{g} \\
  F^{b} \\
\end{pmatrix}
-
\alpha
\begin{pmatrix}
  B^{r}_1 \\
  B^{g}_1 \\
  B^{b}_1 \\
  B^{r}_2 \\
  B^{g}_2 \\
  B^{b}_2 \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

we can rephrase the right side of the equation as:

$$
...
=
\begin{pmatrix}
  1 F^{r} + 0 F^{g} + 0 F^{b} - \alpha B^{r}_1\\
  0 F^{r} + 1 F^{g} + 0 F^{b} - \alpha B^{g}_1\\
  0 F^{r} + 0 F^{g} + 1 F^{b} - \alpha B^{b}_1\\
  1 F^{r} + 0 F^{g} + 0 F^{b} - \alpha B^{r}_2\\
  0 F^{r} + 1 F^{g} + 0 F^{b} - \alpha B^{g}_2\\
  0 F^{r} + 0 F^{g} + 1 F^{b} - \alpha B^{b}_2\\
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
  1 & 0 & 0 & - B^{r}_1 \\
  0 & 1 & 0 & - B^{g}_1 \\
  0 & 0 & 1 & - B^{b}_1 \\
  1 & 0 & 0 & - B^{r}_2 \\
  0 & 1 & 0 & - B^{g}_2 \\
  0 & 0 & 1 & - B^{b}_2 \\
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
	F^{r} \\
	F^{g} \\
	F^{b} \\
	\alpha \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Substituting this into the previous equations gives us the final result of

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
  C^{r}_1  -B^{r}_1 \\
  C^{g}_1 -B^{g}_1 \\
  C^{b}_1 -B^{b}_1 \\
  C^{r}_2 -B^{r}_2 \\
  C^{g}_2-B^{g}_2 \\
  C^{b}_2-B^{b}_2 \\
\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 	0 & 	0 & -B^{r}_1 \\
0 & 	1 & 	0 & -B^{g}_1 \\
0 & 	0 & 	1 & -B^{b}_1 \\
1 & 	0 & 	0 & -B^{r}_2 \\
0 & 	1 & 	0 & -B^{g}_2 \\
0 & 	0 & 	1 & -B^{b}_2 \\
\end{pmatrix} 
\begin{pmatrix}
F_r \\
F_g \\
F_b \\
\alpha
\end{pmatrix}
$$
\end{center}
